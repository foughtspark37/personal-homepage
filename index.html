Background

Finance professional based in Columbus, Ohio with end-to-end private fund experience from origination and due diligence to performance reporting and fund management. Specialize in partnering directly with general partners and private market leadership teams across private equity, private credit, commercial real estate, and venture capital funds ranging from $100M to $10B assets under management.

• Investments: Worked with private equity search funds to source and evaluate mature, industrial businesses in the southern US

• Investor Relations: Successfully raised pre-seed/seed stage rounds to fuel product development

• Fund Operations: Currently at Juniper Square, I partner with General Partners across the private market to streamline and manage their funds' capital activity and investor communications over its lifecycle while navigating complex fund structures.

My work sits at the intersection of fund operations and client partnership, translating complex LP agreements into accurate waterfall models, managing capital call and distribution workflows, and helping GP teams navigate the financial mechanics that underpin their investor relationships.
